About Me

Hi, I’m David Demean — a passionate Web Penetration Tester and Bug Bounty Hunter with a focus on application security. I have experience in testing the security of web and API applications for well-known companies across the globe. In addition to my work as a penetration tester, I also operate as an independent Security Researcher at HackerOne, helping companies secure their online assets.

I am highly skilled in identifying vulnerabilities in applications and have a strong understanding of the OWASP Top 10 Web Application Security Risks. I am constantly learning and staying up to date with the latest technologies and offensive security techniques in the field.
